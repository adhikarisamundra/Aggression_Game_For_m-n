How to Play

Phase 1: Placement

Players take turns placing troops. Stack troops to create strongholds.

Phase 2: Attack

Rule: Player who placed the very first troop attacks first.

If Sum of Your Surrounding Troops > Enemy Stack, enemy is removed.

AI Personalities

Weak: Random. Strategic: Clusters. Smart: Calculates Threat.
