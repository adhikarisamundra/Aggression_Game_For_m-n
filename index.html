MISSION PARAMETERS

Win Conditions

  • • Area Control: Occupy more tiles than the enemy.
  • • Total Annihilation: Wipe the enemy off the board.
  • • Tiebreakers: If tiles are equal, the player with more troops in reserve wins.

Combat Mechanics

Placement is everything. If you place a unit next to an enemy with fewer troops, their unit is instantly destroyed.
