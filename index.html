GAME PROTOCOLS

Phase 1: Placement

Players take turns placing troops. You can place multiple troops on a single tile to create strongholds. Constraint: You cannot place troops on a tile that is already occupied.

Phase 2: Attack

The player who placed the very first troop attacks first. Click an enemy tile to attack.
The Math: Add up ALL your troops surrounding the target. If Sum > Enemy Stack, target is removed.

AI Personalities

  • Weak AI: Moves completely randomly.
  • Strategic AI: Groups troops (clustering) and targets large stacks.
  • Smart AI: Calculates threat levels, simulates future moves, and builds defensive walls.
