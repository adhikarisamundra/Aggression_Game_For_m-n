How to Play

Phase 1: Placement

Players take turns placing troops.

• You can place multiple troops on a single tile.

• Tip: Stack troops to create strongholds.

Phase 2: Attack

Rule: The player who placed the very first troop attacks first.

• Click an enemy tile to attack.

• Math: Sum of YOUR surrounding troops vs. Enemy Stack.

• If Your Sum > Enemy Stack, target is removed.

AI Types
  • Weak: Random moves. Good for practice.
  • Strategic: Clusters troops and attacks big stacks.
  • Smart: Calculates threats and defends vulnerable spots.
