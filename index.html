AGGRESSION: DISCRETE CONQUEST

AXIOMS OF ENGAGEMENT

The game board functions as a Coordinate Plane where each node $(x,y)$ can host a scalar value (Troops).

Neutralization occurs when a placement $V_1$ is adjacent to $V_2$ and $V_1 > V_2$. This results in the immediate removal of the lesser value.

VICTORY CONDITIONS

  • • Area Dominance: Possession of $>50\%$ of available coordinate nodes.
  • • Resource Exhaustion: Opponent lacks remaining scalar units for legal moves.
  • • Efficiency Tiebreaker: If nodes are equal, the higher total sum of unit reserves wins.
